# DPO Master Document Checklist — Bara Afrika

**Last updated:** 2026-02-24

This checklist is the complete list of PDFs (and source documents) you need to compile and email for:
- Data Controller registration
- Authorization to **Store** personal data outside Rwanda
- Authorization to **Transfer** personal data outside Rwanda

Submission instruction (from the DPO forms):
- Convert all documents to **PDF**, then **ZIP**
- Email to:
  - `registration@dpo.gov.rw`
  - `dpp@ncsa.gov.rw`
- Email subject: **your organization name**

## 1) Core documents to DOWNLOAD (official DPO forms)

### 1.1 Data Controller Registration Form (DOCX)
- Download:
  - https://dpo.gov.rw/fileadmin/DPO/Services/Data-Controller-Registration-Form.docx
- Output PDF you will send:
  - `DPO_Form_Data_Controller_Registration.pdf`

### 1.2 Authorization to STORE personal data outside Rwanda (PDF form)

- Output PDF you will send:
  - `DPO_Form_External_Storage_Authorization.pdf`

### 1.3 Authorization to TRANSFER personal data outside Rwanda (PDF form)
- Download:
  - https://dpo.gov.rw/fileadmin/DPO/Services/application-form-for-external-transfer-authorization.pdf
- Output PDF you will send:
  - `DPO_Form_External_Transfer_Authorization.pdf`

### 1.4 DPO Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) (PDF)
- Download:
  - https://dpo.gov.rw/fileadmin/DPO/ComplianceTools/Standard%20Contractual%20Clauses%20for%20Transfer%20Personal%20Data%20Outside%20Rwanda_.pdf
