# Privacy Policy (Draft) — Bara Afrika

**Last updated:** 2026-02-24

This Privacy Policy explains how Bara Afrika (“Bara”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, shares, and protects personal data when you use our website, applications, and services (the “Services”).

> Note: This is a draft for review and should be adapted to match your final product behavior, hosting locations, and vendor list.

## 1. Who we are
- **Controller (company):** Bara Afrika
- **Contact:** privacy@baraafrika.com (recommended), or business@baraafrika.com

## 2. What personal data we collect
Depending on how you use the Services, we may collect:

### A) Account and profile data
- Name (first/last)
- Email address
- Phone number (if provided)
- Profile photo
- Country/city (if you choose it)

### B) Content you submit
- Reviews (rating, title, content)
- Marketplace listings (title, description, images, price, contact details you include)
- Event-related content (registrations, comments, photos)
- Streams-related actions (likes, playlists, play history)

### C) Usage and device data
- Pages viewed, clicks, and interactions
- IP address and approximate location (derived from IP)
- Browser/device identifiers
- Log data (timestamps, error logs)

### D) Communications
- Messages you send to support
- Email preferences and delivery status

## 3. Why we collect data (purposes)
We use personal data to:
- Provide and operate the Services (accounts, listings, reviews, events, marketplace)
- Authenticate users and prevent fraud/abuse
- Personalize content (e.g., country/city relevance)
- Maintain safety and integrity (moderation, reporting)
- Measure performance and improve our Services
- Communicate with you (service messages, updates)
- Process purchases where applicable (coins, boosts, premium placements)

## 4. Legal basis (high-level)
We process personal data based on one or more of the following:
- **Consent** (e.g., optional marketing)
- **Contract/Service performance** (providing the Services you request)
- **Legitimate interests** (security, analytics, product improvement)
- **Legal obligation** (when required)

## 5. How we share personal data
We may share data with:

### A) Service providers (processors)
Examples (adjust to your actual stack):
- Authentication and identity management (e.g., Clerk)
- Database and hosting (e.g., Supabase)

### C) Legal and safety
We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users and the platform.

## 6. International transfers / cross-border storage
Your personal data may be stored or processed in countries outside Rwanda depending on where our vendors host infrastructure.

If we transfer/store personal data outside Rwanda, we will use appropriate safeguards (including contractual safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable).

## 7. Data retention
We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described, including:
- Account data: retained while your account is active
- Logs: retained for security and debugging for a limited period
- Financial/payment records: retained as required by law

We will define exact periods in an internal retention schedule.

We use technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal data, such as:

- Monitoring and logging
- Secure vendor selection

No system is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials secure.

- Request access to your personal data

To make a request, contact: privacy@baraafrika.com.
